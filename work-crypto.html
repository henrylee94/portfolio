Crypto payment rails — two years, three wallet protocols

Letting users pay in crypto — with numbers that are never off.

OMNI · ERC-20 · TRC-20 2-engineer pair, 2 years exact-decimal balances

Situation

The platform needed cryptocurrency deposits and withdrawals alongside fiat — conversion at every transaction boundary, on a platform where some third-party providers couldn't hold crypto at all.

Design

I drove the payments side for two years as half of a two-engineer pair (alongside the team's assistant lead): wallet protocol integrations (OMNI, ERC-20, TRC-20), conversion embedded at transfer boundaries, provider capability as per-provider configuration — and exact-decimal handling at the wallet edges, where a rounding error becomes a real discrepancy.

Outcome

Crypto rails went live across every payment flow; later currencies reused the same config-driven paths instead of re-opening them.
